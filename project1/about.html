About me

Background

I’m passionate and aspiring web designer who loves everything about the web. I'm originally from Argentina but I live in Hawaii and travel around many places. I’m excited to bring my life experience to the process of building many different fantastic-looking websites.

I feel web-design has infinite possibilities. Let's make our imagination fly and stand-out making a unique experience.

Goals

I want to master the process of building web sites and increase my knowledge, skills and abilities in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Ajax
  • Photoshop

I’d like to work for a web design firm helping clients create an impressive online presence or help anyone to start their business or create a better experience by re-designing webs.
